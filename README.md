This is a large CK3 modlist refined through many hours of gameplay, designed to keep the game balanced, stable, and consistently fun across long campaigns.

It stays close to vanilla, but enriches it with stronger historical, dynastic, cultural, and military flavor while keeping the experience believable and grounded without turning the game into something that feels disconnected from its setting.

Important Notes 📜
The current focus of the modlist is Europe and the Middle East. More regional flavor for Asia and Africa will come later 🌍

Recommended start: Kaiser Heinrich IV of the Holy Roman Empire (1066). This campaign quickly exposes many systems used in the modlist such as court management, vassal politics, religion, and warfare.

If you use both Court Herbalist and Improved Witchcraft, you must have the Folk Healers tradition for the court position to appear.

Immersive Siege Battles is still under testing and may conflict with mods that modify fort levels or defensive building mechanics.

Travelers adds a lot of immersion but is also a sensitive mod. It remains included while different situations are being tested.

Large overhaul mods such as Dark Ages, Chat GPT Flavor Pack, and Immersive Domain Management were tested during development. These mods introduce large gameplay shifts or additional layers of complexity that move away from the original direction of this modlist, and can also introduce stability risks in large setups, so they are intentionally not included.

Recommended Game Rules ⚙️
More Lifestyles > New Lifestyles: Secondary Lifestyles
More Lifestyles > Passive Prowess Experience: Low
More Lifestyles > Passive Leadership Experience: Low
Historicity > Diplomatic Range: Restricted
Tweaks > Exclave Independence: Total (optional, helps reduce bordergore sometimes)

Recommended Graphics Settings 🖥️
Maximum FPS: 60
Limiting FPS stopped my PC from overheating and prevented system crashes.
Lower shadows if needed
